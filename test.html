  • Welcome to the International Blockchain Olympiad 2022!

    International Blockchain Olympiad (IBCOL) is an annual competition inviting current and recent students to propose solutions to real-world problems using blockchain technology.

    IBCOL combines the practicality of a traditional case competitions with the open-ended problem-solving culture of hack-a-thons. As a team of up to 6 students, competitors will submit their case as using an academic conference style white-paper along with a pitch and presentation deck.
